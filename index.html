About Me

Hi, I'm Parth Natekar, a Dual Degree (Bachelor's + Master's) student in the Department of Engineering Design at the Indian Institute of Technology, Madras.
My research interests include Human-Computer Interaction, Machine Learning & Data Science, Mechanical & Electrical Design, and Biomedical Design.
Through my research, I would like to bridge the gap between human beings and the growing array of computers and Artificial Intelligence around us.
I believe that augmenting human beings to allow easier interaction with machines and making AI easier to trust are the two main ways to do this. I am also interested in applying ML/AI/Biomedical Design to create systems that can aid medical professionals to diagnose and treat people.

My hobbies include playing football (I'm a Liverpool fan), painting (See the Art Section), and working out.
