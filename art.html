​Replica of Cafe Terrace by Night,
Vincent Van Gogh

I've always been fascinated by impressionist paintings. This is my
attempt to recreate one of Van Gogh's most famous paintings,
​Cafe Terrace by Night, which he painted in Arles, France in 1777.

Oil on Canvas, 16" x 24"
November 2018

​The Road Not Taken

In his famous poem, The Road Not Taken, Robert Frost says,
"Two roads diverged in a yellow wood, and I -
I took the one less traveled by".
This impressionist palette knife painting is inspired
by these lines.

Oil on canvas, 16" x 16"
​December 2017
